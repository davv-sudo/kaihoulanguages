About

Kaihou Languages supports open-source projects through language and communication.

We help developers and small teams overcome language barriers with translation, interpreting, and simple tools.

Focused on clarity, accessibility, and real-world impact.

Services

Translation

README files, documentation, project websites, UI text, and developer guides.

Interpreting

Simple meetings, remote discussions, and collaboration between small international teams.

Tools

Chatbots for FAQs, Telegram support bots, and lightweight communication helpers.

Model

Our services are completely free for open-source projects.

Support is welcome but never required.

This is sustained through affordable work with private clients.
